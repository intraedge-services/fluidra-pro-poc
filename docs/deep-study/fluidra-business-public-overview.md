## 3. Integrated Business Model

Fluidra operates an integrated value chain covering R&D, product design, manufacturing, distribution, and (for the Commercial Business Unit) project management. This vertically integrated model delivers end-to-end service. ([source](https://www.fluidra.com/projects/business-model/))

### Market Context
- Approximately **19 million pools worldwide**
- Fluidra manufactures and/or distributes practically all components for pool construction, renovation, improvement, and maintenance
- Strong position in both **new construction** and **aftermarket** (service, renovation, replacement)

([source](https://www.fluidra.com/about-fluidra/business/))

## 4. Fluidra Pro — The Dealer Platform

### 4.1 What is Fluidra Pro?

Fluidra Pro ([fluidrapro.com](https://www.fluidrapro.com)) is the B2B digital platform serving pool professionals. It provides products, solutions, and support for managing and growing their businesses — from business software for day-to-day operations to personalized loyalty programs with rewards on each purchase. ([source](https://www.fluidrapro.com/en-ca/))

### 4.2 Who Uses Fluidra Pro?

| Professional Type | What They Do |
|------------------|--------------|
| **Pool Builders** | Design and construct new residential/commercial pools |
| **Service Professionals** | Maintain, repair, and renovate existing pools |
| **Retailers** | Sell pool equipment and chemicals through storefronts |
| **Commercial Service** | Maintain pools at hotels, resorts, municipalities |
| **Internet Sellers** | Online pool equipment retailers |

### 4.3 Value Proposition ("Why Fluidra")

([source](https://www.fluidrapro.com/why-fluidra))

1. **Business Management Tools** — Software to run day-to-day operations
2. **Rewarding Programs** — Trade-tailored programs: earn more, grow more, get more
3. **Technical Support** — Live video stream to technicians for in-field troubleshooting
4. **ProEdge Build** — Job management system for pool builders, by pool builders
5. **Marketing Services** — Full marketing agency with pool industry expertise

## Summary

Fluidra is the world's largest pool and wellness company, serving professionals through its Fluidra Pro platform with loyalty programs (ProEdge, ServicePro, Retail Select), business tools (ProEdge Build, configurators), training (Pro Academy), and technical support. The company operates an integrated model from R&D and manufacturing through distribution to dealer services, supported by a modern data platform unifying dealer relationships across CRM, ERP, and web systems.
